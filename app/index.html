How do we do it

  • Adapted program preparation for UPE

    You will study grammar and vocabulary at the same time. And you will learn how to write essays.

  • Audience and online learning

    The teacher will help not only in the classroom, but also during home preparation. Parents can participate in the chat if they wish.

  • The habit of reading in English

    Articles from sources that are used in the development of this UPE will come to the phone twice a week.

  • Psychological support

    Мы научим тебя методикам прохождения тестирований, умению планировать свою подготовку уверенности в себе.

  • Training on author's manuals

    You don't have to waste time on an independent search for material and nothing to buy additionally. We have compiled 2 handouts for you.

  • Ability to comprehend spoken English

    F.R.I.E.N.D.S won't help you. You will regularly listen and parse audio recordings in accordance with the vocabulary being studied.
